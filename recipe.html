Tomato rice

Tomato rice is one of the easiest dish which adds more spice to your taste

Ingredients

  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Rice
  • Grined spices

Instructions

  1. In a pan heat the oil
  2. Then add cloves,cinnamon,cardamom,bay leaf,cumin
  3. Add sliced onions and green chilies in it and sortey for two mins
  4. After the onion turned golden brown,add ginger garlic paste
  5. Add chopped tomatos,turmeric ,salt in it
  6. Cook until the tomatos turns soft
  7. Add red chilli powder and garam masala
  8. Saute for few mins until the raw smell goes off
  9. Pour 2 cups of water and close the lid
  10. Stir it well
  11. Add drained rice
